# WhatsLauncher

Start conversations instantly on **WhatsApp**, **Telegram**, **Signal**, and **Arattai** — no need to save contacts first.

---

## ✨ What You Can Do

### 💬 Chat on Any App, Instantly
Type a phone number, pick your app, and go. One tap opens a direct conversation — no contact saving required.

### 🌍 200+ Country Codes
Select from a full list of countries with flags and dial codes. Works for any international number.

### 📝 Pre-filled Messages
Write your message once and it auto-fills when the chat opens. Perfect for repeated outreach like support requests, introductions, or follow-ups.

### ⭐ Favorites
Star the numbers you contact most. They stay one tap away, always.

### 📋 Quick Message Templates
Save messages you send often — greetings, follow-ups, directions — and reuse them with a single tap.

### 🕐 Recent Numbers
Your last 10 contacted numbers are saved automatically so you never lose track.

### 📱 Home Screen Widget
Add a widget to your home screen for even faster access — skip opening the app entirely.

### 📎 Clipboard Detection
Copy a phone number from anywhere and WhatsLauncher picks it up automatically.

---

## 📲 Supported Apps

- **WhatsApp** — Direct chat with pre-filled messages
- **Telegram** — Direct chat via native deep link
- **Signal** — Direct chat via secure link
- **Arattai** — Direct chat with pre-filled messages

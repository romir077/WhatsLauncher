# WhatsLauncher

Start conversations instantly on **WhatsApp**, **Telegram**, **Signal**, and **Arattai** — no need to save contacts first.

---

## ✨ What You Can Do

### 💬 Chat on Any App, Instantly
Type a phone number, pick your app, and go. One tap opens a direct conversation — no contact saving required.

### 🌍 200+ Country Codes with Smart Validation
Select from a full list of countries with flags and dial codes. Phone number length is validated per country (e.g. 10 digits for India, 8 for Singapore).

### 📝 Pre-filled Messages
Write your message once and it auto-fills when the chat opens. Works on WhatsApp, Telegram, and Arattai.

### ⭐ Labeled Favorites
Star the numbers you contact most and give them names — "Mom", "Plumber", "Doctor". One tap away, always.

### 📋 Editable Quick Message Templates
Save messages you send often — greetings, follow-ups, directions — and reuse them with a single tap. Edit or delete anytime.

### 🕐 Recent Numbers
Your last 10 contacted numbers are saved automatically so you never lose track. Star any recent to save it as a favorite.

### 📱 Home Screen Widget
Add a widget to your home screen for even faster access — skip opening the app entirely.

### 📎 Clipboard Detection
Copy a phone number from anywhere and WhatsLauncher picks it up automatically.

### 📤 Share Intent
Share text containing a phone number from any app and WhatsLauncher will extract it automatically.

### 🌙 Dark Mode
Full dark theme following WhatsApp's color palette, switches automatically with your system setting.

---

## 📲 Supported Apps

- **WhatsApp** — Direct chat with pre-filled messages
- **Telegram** — Direct chat with pre-filled messages via native deep link
- **Signal** — Direct chat via secure link
- **Arattai** — Direct chat with pre-filled messages

WhatsLauncher - Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 22, 2026

Introduction

WhatsLauncher ("we", "our", "the app") is a utility application that helps users start conversations on messaging apps without saving contacts. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Data We Collect

We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. All data stays on your device.

The app stores the following information locally on your device only:

  • Recent numbers — the last 10 phone numbers you contacted
  • Favorites — phone numbers and optional labels you save
  • Message templates — quick messages you create

This data is stored using Android SharedPreferences and never leaves your device.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • No personal information (name, email, address)
  • No location data
  • No contacts or address book access
  • No analytics or tracking
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No crash reports or diagnostics sent externally
  • No account creation required

Clipboard Access

The app reads your clipboard when opened to detect copied phone numbers for convenience. This data is processed locally and is never transmitted or stored beyond the current session.

Third-Party Services

WhatsLauncher opens conversations in third-party messaging apps (WhatsApp, Telegram, Signal, Arattai) using standard Android deep links. Once you leave WhatsLauncher, the respective app's privacy policy applies. We do not control or have access to data in those apps.

Children's Privacy

WhatsLauncher does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. The app does not require account creation and stores no personal data externally. For our child safety standards, please visit our Child Safety Policy.

Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete it at any time by:

  • Clearing the app data from Android Settings
  • Uninstalling the app

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

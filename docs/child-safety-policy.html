WhatsLauncher - Child Safety Standards

Last updated: March 22, 2026

Our Commitment

WhatsLauncher is committed to the safety and protection of children. We have zero tolerance for child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) content or behavior on our platform.

About WhatsLauncher

WhatsLauncher is a utility app that allows users to open conversations on messaging apps (WhatsApp, Telegram, Signal, Arattai) without saving phone numbers to their contacts. The app does not host, store, or transmit any user-generated content, messages, images, or media. All conversations happen within the respective third-party messaging platforms.

Standards Against CSAE

  • WhatsLauncher does not provide any messaging, media sharing, or social networking functionality.
  • The app does not store, process, or transmit any user content beyond locally saved phone numbers and message templates.
  • No user-to-user communication occurs within WhatsLauncher.
  • We do not collect or store personal data of minors.
  • Any CSAE content discovered in connection with our app will be immediately reported to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) and relevant local authorities.
